T3 Half-Swing Special Scholarship Golf Outing April 11, 2022

This scholarship fund has been established by the Reilly family to honor a Father, Son, and Husband - Thomas J. Reilly III, Class of 1976 - who passed April 16, 2021.

Contributions provide scholarships and tuition assistance to students at Holy Cross Preparatory Academy. Tom Reilly - known to his friends as "T3" - was the middle segment of three generations that either attended or supported Holy Cross High School for decades.

Tom was a long time coach of youth sports and much of his time and generosity was spent supporting Catholic Education.
